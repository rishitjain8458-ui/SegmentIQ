# SegmentIQ

**Know your customers. Deeply.**

RFM-based customer segmentation tool — upload your transaction data, get instant customer intelligence.

🔗 **Live:** [rishitjain8458-ui.github.io/SegmentIQ](https://rishitjain8458-ui.github.io/SegmentIQ/)

---

## What It Does

Scores every customer on **Recency, Frequency, and Monetary value** — then automatically places them into one of 8 segments: Champions, Loyal, Potential, New Customers, At Risk, Can't Lose, Hibernating, and Lost.

From there it shows you exactly who to retain, who to win back, and where your revenue is actually coming from.

---

## Features

- RFM scoring for every customer
- 8 automatic customer segments
- Segment distribution and revenue breakdown charts
- Top 10 most valuable customers
- At-risk customer detection
- 6 strategic recommendations based on your data
- Pareto analysis — revenue concentration
- 3-page PDF export report

---

## How To Use

Upload a CSV with three columns — `customer_id`, `purchase_date`, `amount` — and click Run Analysis. Optional columns: `customer_name`, `product_category`, `city`.

No sample data? Click **Use sample dataset** to see a live demo instantly.
